Admission Procedure

Photograph of student and parents duly attested by a gazetted officer For residence proof any of the following documents are applicable

  • .

    Ration card

  • .

    Electricity bill

  • .

    Phone bill

  • .

    Municipal Tax Receipt

  • .

    Gram Panchayat Certificate

  • .

    Residence certificate from Tehsildar

  • .

    Aadhar Card

  • .

    Voter ID Card

Please note that the residence should be within 1km radius from the school for which a Google map printout duly attested should be attached.

For Proof of age, the following shall be considered:
  • .

    Birth Certificate

  • .

    Register record of the hospital (ANMs)

  • .

    Anganbadi Record

  • .

    Declaration of the age of the child by parent or guardian shall be acceptable

  • .

    For disabled candidate, a certificate by Chief Medical Officer shall be acceptable

Proof of EWS:
  • .

    BPL Card

  • .

    For disabled candidate, a certificate by Chief Medical Officer shall be acceptable

  • .

    Application should reach the office of the school not later than 10/02/2021

Admission Policy:

For Children from Disadvantaged Groups and Weaker sections.

  • .

    25% of the total seats for Nursery section are blocked for the children from disadvantaged groups and weaker section from the neighborhood within a radius of 1km.

  • .

    15% of the seats shall go to disadvantaged group and 10% to children belonging to weaker section.

  • .

    Selection will be done on a random lottery bases if the number of candidates exceeds the seats available.
