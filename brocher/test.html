Our Approach : How we achieve it

ABC Public School is affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education(CBSE), New Delhi. The best CBSE affiliated in area offers Secondary and Senior Secondary programs of CBSE across all disciplines. The Board has declared ABC Public School as a New Generation School with the affiliation number –1122334

The CBSE is a national level board of education in India, for public and private schools, controlled and managed by the Union Government of India and follows the National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) curriculum. It envisions a robust, vibrant and holistic school education that engenders excellence in every sphere of human endeavor. The Board is committed to provide quality education to promote intellectual, social and cultural vivacity among its learners. It works towards evolving a learning process and environment, which empowers the future citizens to become global leaders in the emerging knowledge society. The Board advocates holistic development of learners and commits itself to providing a stress-free learning environment that will develop competent, confident and enterprising citizens who will promote harmony and peace

It is an eminent board of school education in India which helps in raising the academic standards of millions of children of our country. Safety and Security : How we take car

ABC Public School, is synonymous to the safest haven for the staff and students alike. Here, it is imperative that everyone feels safe and secure at all times. Mock Evacuation Drill is conducted at regular intervals where the students and staff are required to evacuate the school building in 5 minutes. It is processed to deal with any unprecedented circumstances. Punching in the RFID card in buses and at the campus also ensures safety of all the students. That's a mandate all the students and staff members follow on daily basis. To strengthen the safety ofthe students while travelling to the fro, a guard is appointed in the buses to assist the students.
